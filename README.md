## FAQ

**How do I start a workout?**
Tap the Workout tab and select your workout type, then start a session on your Apple Watch.

**How do I connect Apple Health?**
On first launch, tap "Grant Health Access" and approve all permissions in the Health app.

**My heart rate isn't showing during a workout — what do I do?**
Make sure your Apple Watch is snug on your wrist and the workout is started from the watch.
