# Privacy Policy for Zonovo

Last updated: June 27, 2026

we have built the Zonovo app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection and use of personal data.

 ##Data We Collect
- **Health & Fitness Data**: heart rate, sleep, HRV, workouts — read from Apple Health on your device. This data never leaves your device except to sync with your own account.
- **Account Data**: your name and email via Google Sign-In.
- **Usage Data**: collected via Firebase for app stability.

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide personalized training metrics and AI coaching
- To sync your data across your devices via Firebase
- We do not sell your data to third parties

## Apple HealthKit
We access Apple Health data solely to provide fitness features. We do not share HealthKit data with third parties or use it for advertising.
